What is HTML5UP?

HTML5UP is a website created by n33 which offers a variety of free templates. The templates are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 License.

Recommended for: Anything

The HTML5UP templates provided here are classified as "Recommended for: Anything" in Pixelarity, another templates website by n33 that offers attribution-free versions of HTML5UP templates and more.
